About Us

The story behind Origins.

This website was created from a love of art and its ability to connect people across time and cultures. We realized that art history often feels distant or intimidating, and we wanted to change that. Whether youâ€™re an artist, a student, or someone curious about the stories behind great works of art, this is a space to explore, learn, and be inspired. Art is for everyone, and so is this website.


This is represented through our three essential values:

Inspiration

We encourage users to explore the timeless relevance of historical masterpieces by drawing connections to their own lives, experiences, and creative journeys. Art has the power to transcend time and culture, offering insights and inspiration that resonate deeply with modern audiences.

Community

We celebrate the diverse voices, cultures, and movements that have shaped art history worldwide. By highlighting perspectives from ancient civilizations to contemporary creators, we foster connection and understanding. Join us in exploring the global tapestry of art, celebrating its shared humanity and rich diversity.

Accessibility

We believe art history should be open to everyone, regardless of background or education. By using clear language, engaging content, and interactive tools, we aim to make the stories and significance of art history approachable, inspiring, and inclusive for all.

Where are we Based?

Based in the vibrant city of Manchester, we celebrate its rich artistic heritage while showcasing inspiration and work from artists across the UK and around the world. Our platform connects local and global creativity, making art accessible and engaging for all.
